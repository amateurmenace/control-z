Roadmap

The homepage only shows tools that run. This page is everything else we intend to build — honestly labeled as intent, sequenced by what communities need and what our shared engines make cheap next. Tell us what to build first in the discussions on GitHub.

Building now

Pivot · in progress

Auto-reframe your 16:9 masters to 9:16, 1:1, 4:5 — with an editor's eye, not a cropping algorithm's.

 Stencil · in progress

Click an object once, get a matte for the whole shot — delivered as matte clips any page of free Resolve can use.

 Scribe · in progress

Local transcription with speakers, captions, and the paper-edit superpower: select text, get a cut timeline back.

 Clear · in progress

Voice isolation, de-hum, de-click, room-tone matching, and loudness to spec — the RX-shaped hole in every community edit.

 Rise · in progress

SD→HD/4K reconstruction for tape-era archives and punch-in rescue — with a heatmap of what it invented.

 Depth · in progress

A depth matte for every clip — fog, rack focus, and depth-graded atmosphere through a paste-in Fusion template pack.
