tools we recommend instead of rebuilding

The rest of the free stack.

control-z only builds what doesn't exist for free. These already exist, are already excellent, and belong in every kit — we link them instead of cloning them:

  • HandBrake — transcoding.
  • Shutter Encoder — the swiss-army converter (ProRes proxies, rewraps, burn-ins).
  • LosslessCut — instant trims without re-encoding.
  • OBS Studio — capture and streaming.
  • Audacity — quick audio surgery.
  • Natron — node compositing (and it runs OpenFX, so Hush works there too).

From our own shop, outside the suite: community-captioner (free browser-based live captions for OBS — Scribe's live sibling) and the BIG download/ingest utilities. Ask at Community AI.
